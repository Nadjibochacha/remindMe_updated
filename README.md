üìò User Manual: How RemindMe Works
The core philosophy of RemindMe is Proactive Exposure. Instead of waiting for you to open the app, the app brings the knowledge to you.

Step 1: Create a Collection
Purpose: Group related words together (e.g., "Medical Terms," "French Verbs").

Action: Tap the + icon, name your collection, and choose a color-coded theme.

Step 2: Add Your Content
Input: Type in the word/phrase and its definition or translation.

Storage: Your data is saved instantly to our ObjectBox high-speed local database.

Step 3: Set the "Nudge" Frequency
Customization: Go to the collection settings and choose your interval (e.g., every 1 hour).

The Magic: The app's background scheduler picks a random word from your active collection and pushes it to your notification tray.
